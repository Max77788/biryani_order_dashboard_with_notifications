We use the information we collect to process your orders and to send you promotional information, should you choose to receive it. We take your privacy seriously and will not share your information with third parties.

Your personal information is important to us. We store your data on protected servers and take reasonable measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.
